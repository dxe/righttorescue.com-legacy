DxE investigators documented cannibalism, infections, and death inside Pleasant Valley Farms, a Costco “cage-free” egg farm. Now, investigators Jason and Paul are being charged over $330,000 in restitution, and DxE is being sued by Costco. Costco claims to give animals 5 freedoms, including freedom from pain and suffering, and the company received widespread public praise for committing to selling only cage-free eggs. But DxE’s exposé showed the world the horrific reality behind the “cage-free” label, in the Washington Post, New York Times and more. Jason and Paul are currently appealing the restitution charges.
