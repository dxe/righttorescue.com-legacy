Do you dream of a world where all the slaughterhouses are gone? At DxE, we don’t wait for the dream, we bring the dream to life. We walk into slaughterhouses and farms, and we take the animals out. We have an incredible team of people who are working day and night to expose criminal corporations and rescue injured animals from abuse. From the whistleblowers who risk their freedom by exposing animal abuse to the legal team that brings the stories of rescued animals to the courts, DxE is fighting for animal liberation, starting with the #RightToRescue animals. Our defendants are going to court and if they win this groundbreaking case, we can set a precedent that every suffering animal deserves to be rescued and get closer to a world where all animals are safe, happy, and free.

We have the people, the power, and the passion to win in court. But there’s one missing piece our team needs. Watch the video and find out what it is.
